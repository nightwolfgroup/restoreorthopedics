Navigating the Content Management System

Logging In

Open a web browser (such as Safari or Google Chrome) and navigate to:

https://restoreorthobiologic.com/dashboard

Enter your username and password

Finding Your Way Around

Here is the Home Screen of your CMS Dashboard

Navigation

Navigation Items

  • Pages

    You can control the content of all website pages with this menu

  • Messages

    Messages sent from patients on the "Contact Us" page will appear here

  • Appointment Requests

    Appointment Requests will appear here

  • Testimonials

    Each "Testimonial" block on the website populates from this section

  • Job Applicants

    If you have a job posted, all applicants will appear here

  • Images

    Manage all website images from this menu

  • Documents

    Manage documents available on the website. New patient forms live here

  • Snippets

    Snippets are small pieces of information displayed on the website

  • Streamforms

    Streamforms allow you to design a form to be submitted by users

  • Analytics

    Here you will find live website analytics pulled from Google

  • Reports

    Reports may be useful if you would like to see an audit log or locked pages

  • Settings

    Here you may add users, edit business information, and edit the navigation
