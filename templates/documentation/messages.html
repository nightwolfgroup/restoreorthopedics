Messages & Appointment Requests

Managing Messages

In addition to being shown here, all messages submitted on the website will generate an email that will be sent to admin@restoreorthobiologic.com

Click Messages in the Navigation Menu

Each message will appear in the list

Communicating With Other Staff

Each message has fields for internal communication

From the messages menu, hover over the sender's first name and click EDIT

The field "Staff Notes" can be used by staff for internal communication

The checkbox "Viewed" can be utilized to internally acknowledge that the message has been viewed

To edit or add information to these fields, simply add or change the information and click SAVE at the bottom of the screen

Exporting Messages as a Spreadsheet

Click the button at the top right of the screen Download XLSX

The full list of messages will begin to download to your computer
