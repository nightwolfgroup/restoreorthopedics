Scheduling a Page Publish Date

Scheduling a page publish date might be useful in situations where you want to pre-write blog posts and release them on a schedule.

The nice part about scheduling a publish date, if the page (such as blog pages) includes a date, it will reflect the date the page went live, not the date the page was actually written.

Click Pages in the Navigation Menu then

open the editor for the page you would like to schedule a release date for.

Open the "Page Settings Tab"

Select appropriate date(s) for go-live or expiration of the page

Save your settings by publishing the page
